Xiao

  • Story
  • Talents

He is an adeptus, under the name Alatus, and the sole surviving member of the five foremost Yakshas dispatched by Morax, the God of Liyue, to subdue the demonic spirits that plagued Liyue. He is immensely grateful to Morax for saving him, dedicating his life to protecting Liyue as payment. He does not take lightly to those who speak ill of Morax regardless of whoever they are. Despite suffering from constant karmic debt, Xiao is highly resilient towards its alluring corruption, as his fellow yaksha have either succumbed or died to it. Due to this debt, he prefers being alone and rarely involves himself in mortal affairs; in the few times he does have to, he asks they get to the point for their own sake.
